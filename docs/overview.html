Overview

Our Mission

There is nothing more universal than health. In January 2017, our team came together because we saw a space where growth could be nurtured. Working towards creating a unique product, we hope to better ourselves as engineers/product managers/content producers. Our aim is to exceed the expectations of customers and build a better health-focused product.

Products

The number of Health solutions has exploded in the last few years, with technology ranging from smartwatches to productivity apps. Within such a rapidly developing environment lies an opportunity to take desktop access to new places. The word “Health” indicates a variety of categories – Fitness, Nutrition and Wellbeing (spiritual, mental and emotional) just to name a few. There are more than 165,000 Health apps now available in the Apple iTunes and Android app store.

Customers

For online and mobile technology, millennials lead the way. Now largest demographic in the U.S. as of 2016, they are expected to spend more than $200 billion annually starting in 2017. With such a wide range of experiences within this demographic, we have initially chosen to focus on two subsets:
1. University and College students Between the ages of 18 and 24, this demographic is easily accessible but are already using many different apps. There is an immense opportunity to develop a unique idea. This group are in a period where they are adapting their lifestyle to fit a new environment. Fitness, Nutrition and Wellbeing are all being negotiated and the habits picked up can last a lifetime.
2. Young Professionals Between the ages of 20 and 39, this demographic work in largely white-color industries and have received an associate degree. Described as both relatively affluent and open to early adoption for technology, this group is an attractive target audience.
